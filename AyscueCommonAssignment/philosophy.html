Philosophy of Education

Student-Centered Education

I best align with the Student-Center Philosophy because I feel if you are not teaching to better your teaching, there is no reason to be teaching. I personally believe students have the right to decide who they want to be as people and our jobs as educators is to help them achieve that persona. The reason I fit Student-Center instead a specific philosophy is because just as we intend to shape students to be well-rounded members of society, our philosophy must be stemmed from a variety of ideas. It is also important for your educational philosophy to fit your personality. Young students are receptive when a teacher is not acting genuinely. If we intend to create an environment were students feel safe to be themselves, we must be role models in that regard. I will display this philosophy by attentively analyzing student behavior and success. If I notice falling behind, I will speak with the student to attempt to determine the issue and discuss possible solutions. Similarly, if a student is showing proficiency on their material, I will discuss possible lessons with their parents to provide their child with additional individual attention.
